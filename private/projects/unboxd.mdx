# unboxd

An AI-powered Chrome extension that analyzes product reviews and suggests better alternatives to help you make smarter purchasing decisions.

## Overview

Co-engineered with a focus on solving online shopping decision fatigue, unboxd uses AI to cut through marketing noise and surface genuine product insights.

## The Challenge

Online shopping presents several challenges:
- **Review Overload**: Hundreds of reviews to read through
- **Fake Reviews**: Difficulty identifying authentic feedback
- **Hidden Issues**: Common problems buried in reviews
- **Better Alternatives**: Missing out on superior products

## How It Works

unboxd integrates seamlessly into your shopping experience:

### 1. Automatic Detection
When you visit a product page on supported e-commerce sites, unboxd activates automatically.

### 2. Review Analysis
Our AI analyzes:
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Common complaints and praise
- Product specifications
- Price-to-value ratio

### 3. Smart Insights
Get instant summaries showing:
- **Pros & Cons**: Balanced overview from real users
- **Common Issues**: Recurring problems to watch out for
- **Best Use Cases**: Who this product is actually for
- **Alternative Suggestions**: Better or similar products

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Analysis
- Natural language processing of reviews
- Sentiment analysis for genuine feedback detection
- Pattern recognition for common issues
- Comparative analysis with similar products

### Better Alternatives
- Suggests products with better reviews
- Highlights superior features
- Compares price-to-value ratios
- Shows products you might have missed

### Privacy Focused
- No personal data collection
- On-demand analysis only
- Local storage for preferences
- No tracking or ads

## Technical Stack

Built as a modern Chrome extension:

- **React** - Component-based UI
- **Chrome Extension API** - Browser integration
- **OpenAI GPT-4** - Review analysis and insights
- **Web Scraping** - Product and review data extraction
- **Tailwind CSS** - Responsive popup design
